# Top 3 U.S. Auction Websites (April 2026)

## Ranking source
Based on Similarweb's U.S. Auctions category ranking (March 2026, updated April 1, 2026).

Top 3:
1. hibid.com
2. shopgoodwill.com
3. estatesales.net

## What each does well

### 1) HiBid (hibid.com)
- Strong discovery: auctions by map/state/category/closing soon.
- Multiple bid modes (live, webcast, online-only, absentee).
- Dense marketplace feel with lots, bids, countdown urgency, and hot auctions.

### 2) ShopGoodwill (shopgoodwill.com)
- Personalized buyer account areas (orders, favorites, saved searches).
- Curated sections (Featured, Hot 50, Recommended).
- Trust mission messaging tied to purchases.

### 3) EstateSales.net (estatesales.net)
- Hyper-local search (state or location).
- Notification-led engagement (email alerts for nearby sales).
- Marketplace + service model (find sales, shop items, hire/list companies).

## Combined UX strategy for AUCTIONSITE
- Discovery first: keyword + category + location + ending soon filters.
- Urgency surfaces: countdown timers, bids count, "Hot Lots" lane.
- Personalization: watchlist, saved searches, notifications.
- Trust + clarity: seller badges, pickup/shipping tags, transparent lot cards.
- Dual-sided marketplace: buyers + sellers/auction houses.
